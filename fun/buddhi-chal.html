How to Play:

  • Player 1: Click one of the three buttons (Rock, Paper, or Scissors)
  • Player 2: Click one of the three buttons (Rock, Paper, or Scissors)
  • Rules: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock
  • Winning: First to win 5 rounds wins the game!
  • Tie: If both choose the same, the round is a tie
