Buddhi Chal

Traditional Nepali Strategic Board Game

Player 1
Pieces: 0/3
Player 2
Pieces: 0/3
Phase 1: Place your 3 pieces on the board
Player 1's Turn - Place a piece

How to Play Buddhi Chal:

  • Phase 1: Players alternate placing 3 pieces each on the board intersections
  • Phase 2: After all pieces are placed, players move their pieces along the lines to adjacent intersections
  • Winning: Get 3 of your pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
  • Capture: When you form 3-in-a-row, you can remove one opponent's piece
  • Movement: Pieces can only move to adjacent connected positions
